Our Mission

Our mission is to establish a comprehensive database for neglected and underutilized crops, facilitating access to crucial knowledge to address global challenges. By promoting the cultivation and utilization of these crops, we strive to enhance food security, biodiversity, and sustainability, fostering a resilient future for all.
